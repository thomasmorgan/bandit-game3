Instructions

Across this experiment you will complete rounds, each of which involves trials.


On some trials you may be able to "check" the food sources.

If you check, you won't earn any points on that trial, instead you will see how many points both food sources would have been worth.


Within each round the two food sources are always the same, however between rounds they might change.

If one or both of the food sources do change you will have to learn how temperature affects the new food sources.
