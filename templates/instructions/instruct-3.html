Instructions

Across this experiment you will complete rounds, each of which involves trials.


For the early trials in each round you may be able to "check" the food sources.

If you choose to check, you won't earn any points on that trial, but you will get to see how many points each food source would have been worth.

Checking can help you learn how temperature affects how many points each food source is worth, but remember it will not necessarily be possible to check.


Within each round the two food sources are always the same, however between rounds they might change.

If one or both of the options do change you will have to learn how temperature affects the new options.
