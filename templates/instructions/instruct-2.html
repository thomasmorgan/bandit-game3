The number of points each type of food produces is affected by temperature.

Temperature ranges from extremely hot to extremely cold and to do well you will need to learn how it affects the different food sources.

The payoffs at each temperature are not totally random and so if one option is worth a lot when the temperature is "very hot" it will likely also be valuable when the temperature is "hot" and "extremely hot" though potentially much less valuable at colder temperatures.
